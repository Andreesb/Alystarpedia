Game Tools


Discover a suite of essential game tools designed to enhance your Tibia adventure. From calculating experience and skill progress to upgrading equipment and navigating the world map, each tool offers accurate and user-friendly features to help you optimize your gameplay.

Bontar Bot



Automate routine tasks and streamline your game with Bontar Bot, a Discord bot that helps you manage resources and optimize game actions effortlessly.

Key Finder



Quickly locate and manage your collection of keys using Key Finder, ensuring you never miss unlocking exclusive areas.

Exp Calculator



Plan your leveling journey with the Exp Calculator, providing precise estimates of the experience needed to reach your goal level.

Skills Calculator



Fine-tune your character’s abilities with the Skills Calculator, giving you accurate projections for skill improvements and training milestones.

Exaltation Forge



Upgrade your equipment efficiently using the Exaltation Forge. This tool calculates the gold, dust, and exalted cores needed to boost your items to higher tiers.

Tibia Map



An interactive Tibia Map, which lets you locate any landmarks and plan your adventures.

Loot Splitter



Ensure fair distribution of loot among party members with the Loot Splitter, designed to divide valuable items quickly after a hunt.

Drome



Access Tibia Drome to manage and participate in specialized in-game events, adding an extra layer of strategy to your gameplay.
